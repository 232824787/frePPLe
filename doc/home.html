FREPPLE
a Free Production Planning Library

Plenty of planning tools are available for various production problems, different subprocess, different solver techniques.... A bit suprising, no proper solutions exist in the open source domain. Plenty of users are left with expensive and inflexible tools, and are resorting to spreadsheets to meet their needs.

This project aims at building a lightweight framework that easily and quickly delivers a solution for production planning problems. The initial focus is on small to medium sized master planning problems for the discrete manufacturing industry.

For the developer community, the project is also trying to establish a common ground framework for planning applications. Rather than building the basic building blocks (e.g. item, buffer, bill of material, ...) from scratch over and over again, developers can leverage a proven framework to extend with their own extension modules.

The word "free" in the project name refers to liberty, not price. Think of "freedom of speech" rather than "free beer": see also the free software definition.

The software is currently in a early-beta stage. This means the software isn't suitable for end users yet. There's plenty of work on the code and the documentation. I released at this early stage to collect feedback and to gather interested souls to contribute to the project.
So, browse the site, have a look at the code and let me know what you think.
